# Domain 3: Understanding Trading, Customer Accounts and Prohibited Activities

**Weight**: 31% of exam (approximately 23 questions)

---

## Overview

This domain covers the practical aspects of trading securities, different types of customer accounts, account documentation requirements, and prohibited activities that violate securities laws and regulations.

---

## Key Topics

### 1. Trading and Markets

**Order Types**
- **Market Order**: Execute immediately at best available price
- **Limit Order**: Execute only at specified price or better
- **Stop Order**: Becomes market order when stop price is reached
- **Stop-Limit Order**: Becomes limit order when stop price is reached

**Market Participants**
- **Market Makers**: Provide liquidity, quote bid/ask
- **Specialists**: Maintain orderly market (NYSE)
- **Broker vs. Dealer**
  - Broker: Agent, earns commission
  - Dealer: Principal, earns markup/markdown

**Trade Settlement**
- **Regular Way**: T+2 (trade date + 2 business days)
- **Cash Settlement**: Same day
- **When-Issued (WI)**: Before securities are available

### 2. Customer Accounts

**Account Types**
- **Individual Account**: One owner
- **Joint Account**
  - Joint Tenants with Rights of Survivorship (JTWROS)
  - Tenants in Common (TIC)
- **Corporate Account**: Requires corporate resolution
- **Partnership Account**: Requires partnership agreement
- **Trust Account**: Managed by trustee for beneficiary
- **Custodial Account** (UGMA/UTMA): For minors
- **Retirement Accounts** (IRA, Roth IRA, etc.)

**Account Documentation**
- **New Account Form** - Required information
- **Customer Identification Program (CIP)** - Verify identity
- **Suitability Determination** - Know Your Customer (KYC)
- Account agreements

**Account Features**
- **Cash Account**: Pay in full by settlement
- **Margin Account**: Borrow to purchase securities
  - Regulation T: 50% initial requirement
  - Minimum maintenance: 25%
  - Pattern day trader rules

### 3. Prohibited Activities

**Market Manipulation**
- **Painting the Tape**: False appearance of trading activity
- **Pump and Dump**: Inflate price, then sell
- **Front Running**: Trading ahead of customer orders
- **Wash Sales**: Selling and repurchasing to create tax loss

**Insider Trading**
- Trading based on material non-public information
- Misappropriation theory
- Tipping others

**Fraudulent Practices**
- **Churning**: Excessive trading to generate commissions
- **Unauthorized Trading**: Trading without customer authorization
- **Selling Away**: Selling securities not approved by firm
- **Commingling**: Mixing customer and firm funds
- **Guarantees**: Guaranteeing against loss

**Other Violations**
- **Failure to Supervise**
- **Misrepresentation/Omission** of material facts
- **Interpositioning**: Inserting third party unnecessarily

## Key Regulations

**Regulation Best Interest (Reg BI)**
- Broker-dealers must act in customer's best interest
- Disclosure, care, conflict of interest, compliance obligations

**Know Your Customer (KYC)**
- Reasonable diligence to understand customer
- Financial situation, investment objectives, risk tolerance

**Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**
- Currency Transaction Reports (CTR): >$10,000 cash
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR)
- Customer Identification Program (CIP)

## Account Ownership Rules

| Account Type | Ownership | Rights of Survivorship |
|--------------|-----------|----------------------|
| Individual | One person | N/A |
| JTWROS | Two or more, equal shares | Yes - passes to survivors |
| TIC | Two or more, may be unequal | No - passes to estate |
| Custodial (UGMA/UTMA) | Minor (irrevocable gift) | N/A |
