## A

**Accredited Investor** (合格投资者)
- Individual with $1M+ net worth (excluding primary residence) or $200K+ annual income
- Can participate in private placements

**⭐ ADR - American Depositary Receipt** (美国存托凭证)
- Represents shares of foreign companies
- Trades in U.S. in dollars
- Currency risk applies

**Ask Price** (卖出价/要价)
- Price at which dealer will sell
- Higher than bid price
- Also called "offer"

**At-the-Money** (平价)
- Option's strike price equals current market price
- Example: Stock at $50, option strike at $50

---

## B

**Bear Market** (熊市)
- Market decline of 20% or more
- Pessimistic sentiment

**⭐ Bid Price** (买入价)
- Price at which dealer will buy
- Lower than ask price

**Blue Chip Stock** (蓝筹股)
- Large, established, financially sound company
- Example: Companies in Dow Jones Industrial Average

**⭐ Bond** (债券)
- Debt security
- Issuer borrows money from investor
- Pays interest (coupon)

**Broker** (经纪人)
- Acts as agent
- Earns commission
- Doesn't own the securities

**Bull Market** (牛市)
- Market rise of 20% or more
- Optimistic sentiment

---

## C

**⭐ Call Option** (看涨期权)
- Right (not obligation) to BUY stock at strike price
- Buyer is bullish
- Seller has unlimited risk

**Callable Bond** (可赎回债券)
- Issuer can redeem before maturity
- Usually when interest rates fall
- Investor has reinvestment risk

**Capital Gain** (资本利得)
- Profit from sale of security
- Sale price > purchase price

**⭐ Common Stock** (普通股)
- Ownership in corporation
- Voting rights
- Dividends not guaranteed
- Last in liquidation

**Convertible Bond** (可转换债券)
- Can be converted to common stock
- Conversion ratio specified
- Lower yield than non-convertible

**⭐ Coupon Rate** (票面利率)
- Annual interest rate on bond
- Based on par value
- Fixed for life of bond

**Cumulative Preferred** (累积优先股)
- Missed dividends accumulate
- Must be paid before common dividends

---

## D

**⭐ Dealer** (交易商)
- Acts as principal
- Owns the securities
- Earns markup/markdown

**Debenture** (信用债券)
- Unsecured corporate bond
- Backed only by issuer's credit
- Higher risk than secured bonds

**Dilution** (稀释)
- Reduction in earnings per share
- Occurs when new shares issued

**⭐ Dividend** (股息)
- Distribution of company profits to shareholders
- Declared by board of directors
- Preferred gets priority

---

## E

**⭐ Equity** (股票/权益)
- Ownership interest
- Common and preferred stock

**⭐ ETF - Exchange-Traded Fund** (交易所交易基金)
- Trades on exchange like stock
- Diversified portfolio
- Lower fees than mutual funds

**Ex-Dividend Date** (除息日)
- First day stock trades without dividend
- Must own before this date to receive dividend

---

## F

**Face Value** (面值)
- See Par Value
- Typically $1,000 for bonds

**⭐ FINRA** (金融业监管局)
- Financial Industry Regulatory Authority
- Self-regulatory organization (SRO)
- Regulates broker-dealers

**⭐ Fiscal Policy** (财政政策)
- Government taxation and spending
- Managed by Congress and President

---

## G

**⭐ GO Bond - General Obligation Bond** (一般义务债券)
- Municipal bond backed by taxing power
- Requires voter approval
- Considered safer than revenue bonds

**Growth Stock** (成长股)
- Company reinvests earnings
- Little or no dividends
- Capital appreciation focus

---

## I

**⭐ IPO - Initial Public Offering** (首次公开发行)
- Company's first sale of stock to public
- Primary market transaction

**In-the-Money** (实值/价内)
- Option with intrinsic value
- Call: market price > strike price
- Put: market price < strike price

**⭐ Insider Trading** (内幕交易)
- Trading based on material non-public information
- Illegal
- Subject to penalties

**Interest Rate Risk** (利率风险)
- Risk that rates will rise, causing bond prices to fall
- Greater for long-term bonds

---

## J

**JTWROS - Joint Tenants with Rights of Survivorship** (联名账户-生存者权利)
- Joint account
- Equal ownership
- Passes to surviving tenant(s)

---

## L

**Limit Order** (限价订单)
- Execute only at specified price or better
- May not execute if price not reached

**Liquidity** (流动性)
- Ease of converting to cash
- Stocks more liquid than real estate

---

## M

**⭐ Margin Account** (保证金账户)
- Borrow money to buy securities
- Regulation T: 50% initial requirement
- Minimum maintenance: 25%

**Market Order** (市价订单)
- Execute immediately at best available price
- Guaranteed execution, not price

**Market Risk** (市场风险)
- Risk of overall market decline
- Affects all securities
- Cannot be diversified away

**⭐ Monetary Policy** (货币政策)
- Control of money supply and interest rates
- Managed by Federal Reserve

**⭐ Municipal Bond** (市政债券)
- Issued by state/local governments
- Interest generally tax-exempt
- GO bonds or Revenue bonds

**⭐ Mutual Fund** (共同基金)
- Open-end investment company
- Continuously issues/redeems shares
- Priced at NAV

---

## N

**⭐ NAV - Net Asset Value** (资产净值)
- Value of fund's assets minus liabilities
- Divided by shares outstanding
- Mutual fund pricing

**Nominal Yield** (名义收益率)
- Same as coupon rate
- Annual interest / par value

---

## O

**Odd Lot** (零股)
- Less than 100 shares
- May incur higher costs

**Out-of-the-Money** (虚值/价外)
- Option with no intrinsic value
- Call: market price < strike price
- Put: market price > strike price

---

## P

**⭐ Par Value** (票面价值)
- Face value of bond (typically $1,000)
- For stock: arbitrary value, not market value

**⭐ Preferred Stock** (优先股)
- Fixed dividend
- Priority over common for dividends/liquidation
- Usually no voting rights
- Callable/convertible features

**⭐ Premium** (溢价)
- 1) Bond price > par value
- 2) Cost of an option
- 3) Closed-end fund price > NAV

**Primary Market** (一级市场)
- Where new securities are sold
- IPOs, new bond issues
- Issuer receives proceeds

**Prospectus** (招股说明书)
- Legal document disclosing investment information
- Required for new issues

**⭐ Put Option** (看跌期权)
- Right (not obligation) to SELL stock at strike price
- Buyer is bearish
- Protective strategy

---

## R

**⭐ Regulation T** (T规则)
- Federal Reserve Board regulation
- Sets margin requirements (50%)

**⭐ Revenue Bond** (收入债券)
- Municipal bond backed by project revenue
- No voter approval needed
- Riskier than GO bonds

**Round Lot** (整手)
- 100 shares
- Standard trading unit

---

## S

**⭐ SEC - Securities and Exchange Commission** (证券交易委员会)
- Federal securities regulator
- Created by 1934 Act
- Oversees securities markets

**Secondary Market** (二级市场)
- Where existing securities trade
- NYSE, NASDAQ
- Issuer doesn't receive proceeds

**⭐ Settlement** (结算)
- Final transfer of securities/payment
- Regular way: T+2
- Cash settlement: same day

**Short Sale** (卖空)
- Sell borrowed securities
- Profit if price declines
- Unlimited risk

**⭐ SIPC - Securities Investor Protection Corporation** (证券投资者保护公司)
- Protects customer securities up to $500K (including $250K cash)
- Protects against broker-dealer failure
- NOT insurance against market loss

**Spread** (价差)
- Difference between bid and ask
- Dealer's profit

**Strike Price** (行权价)
- Price at which option can be exercised
- Also called "exercise price"

---

## T

**⭐ T+2** (T+2结算)
- Trade date plus 2 business days
- Regular way settlement for stocks

**TIC - Tenants in Common** (共同共有)
- Joint account
- May have unequal shares
- No rights of survivorship

**Treasury Securities** (国债)
- Issued by U.S. government
- Bills (< 1 year), Notes (2-10 years), Bonds (> 10 years)
- Backed by full faith and credit

---

## U

**Underwriter** (承销商)
- Investment bank that helps issue securities
- Assumes risk in firm commitment
- Agent in best efforts

**⭐ UGMA/UTMA** (未成年人统一赠与法/转移法)
- Custodial accounts for minors
- Irrevocable gift
- Minor gains control at age of majority

---

## V

**Volatility** (波动性)
- Degree of price fluctuation
- Higher volatility = higher risk

---

## W

**⭐ Warrant** (认股权证)
- Long-term right to buy stock at set price
- Often attached to bonds as sweetener
- Trades separately

---

## Y

**Yield** (收益率)
- Return on investment
- Various types: current, yield to maturity, etc.

**⭐ Yield to Maturity (YTM)** (到期收益率)
- Total return if bond held to maturity
- Includes interest and capital gain/loss
